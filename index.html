Welcome to Keys!

Have you ever wondered how to play a tune like “Happy Birthday” on the piano? This is the site for you!
Keys' purpose is to teach simple tunes by guiding you, note by note, through a tune.
As an added feature, you can record your own tune to show off or even teach to others!

Instructions

Rotate your screen if your are using a mobile device.
After clicking on this page you will be taken to the simple piano. There you can play the piano or click on the following buttons:

Select a Tune

Choose your tune, or the "Record your own!" option.

Play a Tune

Click to play the selected tune. Clicking the button again will restart the tune.

Learn the Selected Tune

Follow the displayed notes on the keyboard. If the last note you played was wrong, Keys will repeat the correct note until you get it right.

Record

When “Record your own!” is selected this button will appear. After clicking the button all keys you play will be recorded. To stop recording, click . Your tune is now saved and can be played back or taught just as the preset tunes.

Stop

The stop button will stop any tunes being taught or being played.

Settings

Click on the settings button to open the settings window. Here you can change the sound the piano makes. You can also turn on or off the note names on the piano.

Please Note

The Keys piano works on all modern browsers such as Firefox, Chrome, Opera and Safari. If you are unable to view the piano, consider upgrading your browser.
